DALI is designed as a family-inclusive meeting and every participant is encouraged to bring their families along. There will be activities organised for families, and time for everyone to be together.

Safari

During the long lunch break on Friday, we will take everyone for a real safari experience. We have booked a guided game drive (3h) at Botlierskop (https://www.botlierskop.co.za) where we will hopefully be able to see lions, elephants, zebras, hippos and more.


We will leave the hotel together by bus at 1:00pm (everyone will get a lunch box on the bus) and come back in the evening at 6:00pm giving everybody enough time to take a quick shower and relax before the last keynote of the day and the conference dinner afterwards.

We have booked tickets for all participants and family members. Please let us know if you do not want to or cannot attend.
