Details

Carrer Punta Negra 12, 07181 Costa d'en Blanes, Illes Balears


The H10 Punta Negra is a lovely boutique hotel located on a small peninsular on the Costa d'en Blanes in the south of Mallorca. It is situated in one of the island's most beautiful areas, and offers a fantastic view of the ocean and the nearby marina. The hotel's large landscaped gardens give it a very special character that will allow you to enjoy the peaceful surroundings. Furthermore, guests can also easily visit the island's capital, Palma de Mallorca, which is only 12 km away and was named »the best place to live in the world« by The Times in 2015. Finally, the hotel also has five meeting rooms with natural light and unique coastal surroundings, equipped with everything you need to hold a successful conference.

For more information, please visit the website of the {{ site.conference.venue }}.

How to get there

The closest airport is Palma de Mallorca, which is the only commercial airport on the island of Mallorca. Alternatively, there are also a number of ferries, starting, for example, from Barcelona and Valencia.

From the airport, you can take a taxi (ca. 25 minutes), or use the autobus line A11, which directly goes from the airport to Costa d'en Blanes (stop "H. Punta Negra").
